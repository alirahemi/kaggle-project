# Finanzamt FAQ (synthetic knowledge base)

## Einkommensteuerbescheid

The income tax assessment (*Einkommensteuerbescheid*) shows tax owed or refund due. Compare figures with your submitted tax return.

## Einspruch deadline

You have **one month** after formal notification (*Bekanntgabe*) to file an objection (*Einspruch*). It can be a simple signed letter stating disagreement and reasons.

## Steuernummer vs. Steuer-ID

- **Steuer-ID**: personal lifelong tax ID
- **Steuernummer**: assigned per tax office and case

## Nachzahlung / Erstattung

- *Nachzahlung*: additional tax to pay — check payment deadline and IBAN on the letter
- *Erstattung*: refund transferred to your bank account

## ELSTER

Many filings use [ELSTER](https://www.elster.de) for electronic submission. Keep PDF confirmations.
