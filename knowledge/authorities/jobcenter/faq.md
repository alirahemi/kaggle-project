# Jobcenter FAQ (synthetic knowledge base)

## What is a Nachforderung?

A **Nachforderung** asks you to submit documents that were missing from your Bürgergeld or ALG II application. It is not a rejection — respond before the deadline.

## Common requested documents

- Meldebescheinigung (registration certificate)
- Bank statements (usually three months)
- Lease agreement and proof of rent / utilities
- Employment contracts or termination letters

## What happens if I miss the deadline?

Under § 60 SGB I the Jobcenter may **suspend (ruhend stellen)** your benefits until documents arrive. Contact your case worker (*Sachbearbeiter/in*) immediately if you need more time.

## Where do I submit documents?

Use the address on the letter, your Jobcenter online portal if available, or deliver in person with your **Aktenzeichen** (case number).

## Can I get help?

Migrationsberatung and social counseling centers (*Sozialberatung*) offer free support in many cities. This AI tool does not replace professional advice.
