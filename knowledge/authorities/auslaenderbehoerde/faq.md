# Ausländerbehörde FAQ (synthetic knowledge base)

## Residence permit extension

Apply for an extension **before** your current permit expires — ideally 6–8 weeks prior. Bring passport, biometric photos, Anmeldung, proof of income, and health insurance.

## Fiktionsbescheinigung

If your application is pending, you may receive a **Fiktionsbescheinigung** allowing you to stay legally while the authority decides.

## Termin letters

Appointment (*Termin*) letters specify date, time, and required documents. Missing an appointment can delay your case significantly.

## Language requirements

Some permits require German certificates (A1–B1). Check the checklist in your letter.

## Appeals

Negative decisions (*Ablehnungsbescheid*) include instructions for **Widerspruch** or legal action. Deadlines are strict — often two to four weeks.
