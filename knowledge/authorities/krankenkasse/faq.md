# Krankenkasse FAQ (synthetic knowledge base)

## Mitgliedsbescheinigung

Authorities and employers may request proof of health insurance (*Mitgliedsbescheinigung*). Request it from your Krankenkasse member portal or branch.

## Beitragsbescheid

Contribution notices show monthly premiums. If income changed, notify the Krankenkasse to avoid back payments.

## Mahnung

A **Mahnung** reminds you of unpaid contributions. Pay promptly or contact them to arrange a payment plan (*Ratenzahlung*).

## Family insurance

Family members may be insured at no extra cost if eligibility requirements are met (e.g., income ceiling).

## Switching Krankenkassen

You can switch statutory insurers with proper notice periods. Special termination rights exist after premium increases.
